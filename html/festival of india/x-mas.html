Christmas-Birth of Jesus Christ

 The birth of Jesus Christ is celebrate dwith ecstatic merriment and wonderful joy,throghout India.The warm smell of walnut cakes can be inhaled in every household. As if captivated by a confetti of Santa caps,Christmas sees the prevalence of these caps and reindeer headgear decorating the heads of civilians.many people decorate Christmas trees in their homes with colourful stars and hanging balls. People also visit the Church to seek the holy blessings of Christ on this auspicious festival.
