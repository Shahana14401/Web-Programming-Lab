Diwali-Festival of Lights

 This pan Indian festival,famous for lighting up our lives,Diwali is joyously celebrated globally now.Sending a powerful message of the triumph of good over evil,the festival is celebrated in every nook and corner of the country.People enjoy the festival of lights by beginning of the day with an auspicious pooja,followed by welcoming the night with new traditional clothes,sweets and glowing deeyas.The houses are decorated with garlands of electric lights and colourful rangolis.Ignite the light of brotherhood and goodness in youm being through the festival of Diwali.
